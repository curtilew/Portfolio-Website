Credit Card Optimization

My Credit Card Optimization App helps users maximize rewards by strategically timing credit card applications based on spending patterns, bank rules, and approval likelihood while predicting credit score impacts. I've built personalized application sequencing tools with interactive visualizations that display optimal application windows and projected rewards, transforming credit card optimization from reactive tracking to proactive planning. This innovative approach helps users navigate complex restrictions while maintaining healthy credit scores, addressing a market gap where existing solutions lack forward-looking predictive capabilities.
